Why I write this blog

I know what I am good at, that’s focus.

But everything has two aspects, even if I can focus on one thing for a long period, but due to this reason I can’t balance the other aspect. I was good at many fields, but none of them has kept until now, except my mind. I still keep thinking, stay alone to keep a clear-head.

Even focus can help me achieve many goals, but just in a short period. when time past, when goals achieved. then I will lose interest and desire of it.We all know human’s movement is based on desire, in another words, I stop in some aspect I was good at. why? Why I stopped them? the answer is simple, because I can’t control the environment and when the environment changed, I must modify myself to adapt it.

In one word, I have repeated focus and forget so many times, I was tired of it. Now I want master one field forever, and spend my whole life in it.

I want to use this blog to keep my memory, to record my life.
